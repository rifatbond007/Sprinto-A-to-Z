## What is Sprinto?

**Sprinto** is an AI-native compliance automation platform designed specifically for cloud-native companies, SaaS startups, and modern enterprises. It automates the entire compliance lifecycle—from implementing security controls and managing policies to collecting audit evidence and facilitating certifications.

With Sprinto, companies can achieve and maintain certifications like **SOC 2**, **ISO 27001**, **HIPAA**, **GDPR**, **PCI DSS**, and **40+ other frameworks** with minimal manual effort.

---

## Why It Exists

Compliance is critical for modern SaaS companies. Enterprise customers increasingly require security certifications before doing business. However, traditional compliance processes are:

- **Manual and repetitive** — teams spend months chasing screenshots, updating spreadsheets, and coordinating with auditors
- **Error-prone** — human oversight leads to gaps, missed controls, and audit failures
- **Expensive** — consultants, internal resources, and audit costs add up quickly
- **Reactive** — compliance is treated as a one-time project rather than an ongoing process

Sprinto exists to solve these problems by turning compliance into an **autonomous, continuous process** that keeps companies always audit-ready.

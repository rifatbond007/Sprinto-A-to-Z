## Description

Offers a comprehensive library of auditor-approved security policies that auto-map to compliance frameworks.

- **100+ templates**: Pre-built policies for all major frameworks
- **Auto-mapping**: Policies automatically link to relevant controls
- **Version control**: Tracks policy changes with audit trails
- **Distribution**: Automates policy acknowledgment for employees
- **Review workflows**: Scheduled policy review and approval processes

---

## Supported Policies Include

- Information Security Policy
- Acceptable Use Policy
- Data Classification Policy
- Incident Response Policy
- Business Continuity Plan
- Password Policy
- Access Control Policy
- Vendor Management Policy

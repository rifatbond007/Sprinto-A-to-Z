[← Framework Overview](./README.md) | [Next: Access Control →](./4.2-access-control.md)

---

## Description

Discovers, classifies, and tracks all technology assets across your organization.

- **Cloud asset discovery**: Automatically scans AWS, Azure, and GCP for resources
- **Classification**: Tags assets by sensitivity, owner, and criticality
- **Inventory tracking**: Maintains a real-time register of all systems, applications, and data stores
- **Change detection**: Alerts when new assets are added or modified

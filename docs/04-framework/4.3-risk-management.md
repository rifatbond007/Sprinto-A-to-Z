---

## Description

Provides a centralized framework for identifying, assessing, and mitigating security risks.

- **Risk registers**: Centralized tracking of identified risks
- **Assessment workflows**: Structured risk assessment questionnaires
- **Risk scoring**: Automated scoring based on likelihood and impact
- **Mitigation tracking**: Links risks to controls and tracks remediation
- **Trending analysis**: Visualizes risk posture over time

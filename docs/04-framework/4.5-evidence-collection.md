---

## Description

Automatically pulls, validates, and organizes compliance evidence from connected systems.

- **API-driven collection**: Integrates with 200+ services to pull real-time data
- **Automated screenshots**: Captures configuration states on schedule
- **Evidence linking**: Maps evidence to specific controls
- **Validation**: Checks evidence completeness and freshness
- **Audit packaging**: Exports evidence packages for auditors
